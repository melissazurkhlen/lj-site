City of Lebanon Junction

The Town where the Stories Never End

The city of Lebanon Jucntion is a town deeeply rooted in history.

The town of Lebanon Junciton has went thru many different ups and downs. The town was once a bustling community complete with a hotel, Catholic grade school, high school and grocery store. Once the railroad industry died down the community took a hit. There was a decrease within the population due to a reduction of jobs. Eventually the high school, both groceries and the local florist closed. The flood aslo took a big hit to the community. As more of the older people pass away the town has welcomed in many more renters and there has been a difference in the change of people and atmosphere====.
